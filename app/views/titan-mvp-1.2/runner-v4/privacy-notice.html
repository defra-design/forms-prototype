Privacy notice

This service collects and processes personal information so you can check someone’s form before they submit it.

What information we collect

When you enter your details, we collect:

  • your email address
  • your UK address

Why we collect it

We use your details to record who checked the form and to show the applicant that a named person has completed the check.

How long we keep it

Your details are kept only for as long as needed to support this form-checking journey.

Your rights

You have rights under data protection law, including the right to access your data and ask for it to be corrected or deleted.
